RULE 0

Background Requirements

The background should be still and preferably a dark coloured wall

- the background should not include trees, cloths, or leaves which could move due to wind.

Video Requirements

Place the camera on a tripod or make sure it is still/stationary after the viewport is defined as per the rest of the rules.

The background of the video should be green and in a studio or the video should be recorded on the terrace in ample day-light

To watch the videos make sure you are logged in with your institute email address.

For the submission: Max - resolution = 1920 * 1080, and 30fps. and NO 4k and 60fps videos.

Student has to follow the dress code.

Link to the previous convocation video for dress code reference

RULE 1

VERTICAL MEASURING( Line between Director and Tripod )

Take a five meters tape and mark the starting point , 2 meters point and 5 meters point.

0m is where the director will stand and the 5m is the point camera man will be standing.

2m is the point where we have to draw a long horizontal line and that 2m line will be coinciding with the bottom of the video.

video for reference:

RULE 2

HORIZONTAL MEASURING( Line which student will be following to receive the degree.)

Take a tape and measure the 2m point to the left of the director point.

Draw a line connecting the Director point to the point 2m to the left of the it. And this line will be the used by the student to walk towards the director.

From the Director point mark a point at a distance of 6inches. Draw another point at a distance of 12inches.

student will be starting from the leftside 2m point and he will walk until he reaches the 12inch point.

video for reference :

RULE 3

HOLDING THE CAMERA IN THE CORRECT POSITION.

Stand at the already marked 5m point and hold the camera at a height of 1.2m ( 120cm) from the ground.

After the camera is placed at the correct height and distance, make sure the bottom of the video( visible in the screen) is coinciding with the 2m line and the left most part of the video visible should pass through the left side 2m line.

video for reference :

RULE 4

WALKING TOWARDS THE DIRECTOR TO RECEIVE THE DEGREE.

Start from the left side 2m mark and walk until you reach the 12inch mark.

Total Duration :10sec.

Breaking points.

  • 0sec - start walking.
  • 4sec - namaskar and after that extend you hand to recieve the degree (the hand should be placed in such a way that the centre of the distance between the two hands should like at 120cm( from the ground )) .
  • 5sec - pause the video and take the degree and hold it with your hands as if the director is giving (while continuing the video make sure you stand at the same position when you paused it)
  • 6sec - take a one step ( towards the camera man) and walk towards the right side of the director and continue walking until 10sec.
